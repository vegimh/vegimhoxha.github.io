About Me

As a dedicated mechanical engineer and passionate CrossFit enthusiast, I specialize in designing innovative, high-performance gym equipment. I’ve successfully developed over 20 projects, including the All-in-One m², and have hands-on experience creating custom solutions like racks, pull-up systems, and GHD machines. With a deep understanding of both fitness and engineering, coupled with my role as an assistant coach at Flex Gym Prishtina, I’m motivated to contribute to a team that thrives on creating top-tier, functional equipment. Skilled in CAD design, product development, and manufacturing optimization
